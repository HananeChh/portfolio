Logistics Platform Overview: Streamlining Freight Transport
Optimized Solutions for Carriers and Shippers in Freight Logistics

A comprehensive platform that connects shippers with carriers, ensuring efficient transport of goods.

Carriers:
  • Submit offers on shipments and view all their scheduled deliveries in a clear calendar interface.
  • Manage fleet vehicles efficiently.
  • Access performance statistics and receive real-time notifications for new shipment opportunities.
Shippers:
  • Post detailed shipment requests and receive offers from carriers.
  • Track shipment status at every stage and securely manage payments and invoices.
  • Manage multiple shipments simultaneously with a centralized dashboard for improved oversight.
